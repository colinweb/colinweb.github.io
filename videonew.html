Sherpa Professional Coaching


In 2018, I created 16 "Coach Profiles" and a conference highlight video for Sherpa Professional Coaching LLC. It was my first professional freelance project. I used two cameras and external lights to create intimate profile videos for several Sherpa Coaches at their 2018 EXPO Conference in Cincinnati, Ohio. Here you can see my highlight video that includes shots from coach profiles and other footage from the conference.

Senior Capstone - Found Footage

My senior capstone project for the Electronic Media Program at the UC College Conservatory of Music was completed in spring of 2018. I shot, directed, and edited this 10 minute thriller short film, Found Footage. The project was several months in the making and played at the end of the year E-Media Senior Showcase.
